Ethiopian Institute of Architecture,Building Construction and City Developnent







MISSION AND Vision

Research and academic consultancies are targeted to technology and knowledge transfer for direct application in practice.

EIABC has a Vision of: Architecture, Building Construction and City Development (EiABC)is a home for Architecture, Urbanism and Construction Technology and Management that is focused on educating professionals and providing university-level knowledge to serve Ethiopia’s needs.



EIABC has also Mission: To educate prospective graduates, provide knowledge and develop skills in design, building technology, management, and planning for the field of architecture, the building industry, construction management, as well as city and town actors in the public and private sector.


To conduct technology-based applied research, projects and studies that are based on priorities that reflect the needs of the country, the regions, woredas, kebele and the private business.
